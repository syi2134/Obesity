Obesity Can be Prevented

It is important that you treat obesity seriously because there can be life or death consequences. For example you can have a stroke, heartattack, or you can end up in a wheel chair feeling helpless. Obesity can also cause pschylogical problems which can lead to depression. If you experience any of these symptoms on a daily basis breathlessness, increased sweating, inability to cope with sudden physical activity, feeling tired every day, back and joint pain, low confidence, self-esteem and most importantly feeling isolated you should go to our diet and excercise page to prevent anymore of these symptoms from occuring.
