Obesity
Obesity is a disease where the body has too much adipose tissue which is an organ that increases the levels of circulating fatty acids and inflammation. Basically, its when you have too much body fat. This disease affects millions of adults in America and was associated with over 100,000 deaths yearly.

BMI CALCULATOR

(Weight in Pounds / (Height in Inches)^2) * 703
