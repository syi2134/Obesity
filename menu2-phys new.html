PHYSICAL CAUSES TO OBESITY

AGE
Obesity is possible for people of all ages but the older you are, the higher chance of obesity. As you grow older, hormones change and you tend to follow a less active lifestyle. Muscles also decrease with age thus lowering metabolism.
ACTIVITY/ENVIRONMENT
Exercising burns calories and if you are not very active, you do not burn as much calories. People tend to gain weight easily when someone close to them either family or friend is obese.
DISEASES/MEDICATION
For some people, medical issues may be the cause of obesity. Diseases such as Prader-Willi syndrome, Cushing's syndrome, and other conditions may cause a decrease in activity. Some medications given have also been know to lead to obesity due to its side effects. Medicine such as antidepressants, anti-seizure medications, diabetes medications, etc. can lead to weight gain if you do not compensate through diet or activity.



GENETICS
Genes increase the risk of weight gain and interact with other risk factors such as unhealthy diets and inactive lifestyles.Genes may also affect the amount of body fat you have and where they are distributed. It plays a role in how efficiently your body converts food into energy and how efficiently your body burns calories during exercise.
